      Grading Computation Matrix

      1. Overall Rating Equation

      The final rating is derived using a weighted sum model scaled to a maximum of 5.0:

      Rfinal = (CSacad × 0.10) + (CSlead × 0.25) + (CSpat × 0.25) + (CSsoft × 0.25) + (CStech × 0.15)

      2. Component Score (CS) Matrices

      UPDATED
      Academic (CSacad)

      Evaluated dynamically based on the Mean Average GWA of the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd Semesters. Empty or 0.0 inputs are automatically excluded from the average.

      • Mean GWA ≥ 90.0 → 5.0
      • 85.0 ≤ Mean GWA < 90.0 → 4.0
      • Mean GWA < 85.0 → 3.0

      Star Cadets automatically receive a perfect 5.0 Academic CS regardless of standard brackets.

      Leadership (CSlead)

      Determined by the Cadet's rank and responsibilities assigned during duties.

      • Corps of Midshipmen Officer → 5.0
      • Standard Cadet → 3.0
      Patriotism (CSpat)

      Ratio (P) = Cum. Merits ÷ Benchmark Merits

      • P ≥ 0.75 → 5.0
      • 0.50 ≤ P < 0.75 → 4.0
      • 0.25 ≤ P < 0.50 → 3.0
      • 0.01 ≤ P < 0.25 → 2.0
      • P < 0.01 → 1.0
      Technical Core (CStech)

      Ratio (T) = Passed Tech Tasks ÷ Total Tech Tasks

      • T ≥ 0.90 → 5.0
      • 0.80 ≤ T < 0.90 → 4.0
      • 0.70 ≤ T < 0.80 → 3.0
      • 0.60 ≤ T < 0.70 → 2.0
      • T < 0.60 → 1.0

      3. Soft Skills & Watchkeeping Integration

      The Soft Skills score (CSsoft) is a hybrid metric averaging the completion rate of the Soft Tasks Checklist and the Daily Logbook Submission Rate for active graded months.

      CSsoft = ⌈ (Soft Task Rating + Logbook Rating) ÷ 2 ⌉

      4. Bonus Enhancements & Special Honors

      Star Cadet System

      Reserved for cadets exhibiting outstanding academic excellence.

      • • Condition: Must achieve a 90.0+ Mean GWA across active semesters.
      • • Benefit 1: Grants an automatic perfect 5.0 Academic CS.
      • • Benefit 2: Grants a flat +2.0% Bonus to their final grade.
      Excess Merits Bonus

      Automatically applied when a cadet's total merits exceed the max limit.

      • • Condition: Cumulative Merits exceed the Max Score Cap.
      • • Conversion: Every 10 excess merits directly adds +0.5% to the final specific grade percentage.
      Individual Extra Merits

      Manual override applied by Instructors for extraordinary performance.

      • • Condition: Awarded at the sole discretion of the Intelligence & Security Officer or Dean.
      • • Benefit: The inputted percentage is added directly on top of the calculated final grade.
      Final Specific Grade = Base % Grade + Star Bonus (if applicable) + Individual Bonus %
